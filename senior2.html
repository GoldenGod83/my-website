Expert Helpdesk

Senior Year Project #2

During my senior year, I operated at an expert helpdesk level by job shadowing two IT analysts throughout the year. I received real-world training in managing IT infrastructure at Vinal Technical High School and was trusted with independent responsibilities.

  • Learned about server, local machine, and network management directly from experienced IT professionals.
  • Handled all teacher helpdesk tickets, including software installation, password resets, and troubleshooting connectivity issues.
  • Managed and maintained the entire digital signage system across the school, including:
    • Monitoring TV units and their IP addresses
    • Configuring and syncing Novo DS players to a central laptop
    • Troubleshooting and replacing signage units when they failed
  • Worked independently throughout the building with trust from staff and administrators to resolve technical problems efficiently.

This experience helped me build confidence in enterprise level IT work, troubleshooting under pressure, and understanding how IT supports daily school operations.
