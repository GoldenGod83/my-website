Advanced Helpdesk

Junior Year Project #3

As part of my helpdesk responsibilities during junior year, I supported classroom operations across the school by troubleshooting and resolving technical issues. My focus was on keeping staff devices and classroom setups working smoothly.

  • Assisted teachers with setting up and connecting laptops, desktops, and peripherals.
  • Resolved issues with projectors, Wi-Fi connectivity, and smart board calibration.
  • Managed software installations, account sign-ins, and permission troubleshooting.
  • Brought back computers/devices to IT for further taking apart and fixing.
  • gave on site support in classrooms to reduce downtime during lessons.
