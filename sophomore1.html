Intermediate Helpdesk

Sophomore Year Project #1

During my sophomore year at Vinal Technical High School, I volunteered to handle all helpdesk tickets in the IT trade no matter complexity. This experience gave me real world experience as a beginner IT analyst. The main live recovery OS I utilized during this time was Sergei Strelec's Live Windows Recovery ISO.

  • Troubleshot software and hardware issues for students and staff.
  • Diagnosed network and Wi-Fi problems across multiple devices.
  • Reset user accounts and managed credentials through Google Admin and Microsoft Admin consoles.
  • Documented, communicated, and closed helpdesk tickets efficiently.

This experience shaped my troubleshooting mindset and prepared me for more advanced IT issues later on.
